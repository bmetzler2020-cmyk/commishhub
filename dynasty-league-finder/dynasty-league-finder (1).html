Dynasty League Finder

Dynasty leagues are a 10-year commitment.
Finding one shouldn't take that long.

The current experience is embarrassing

A Reddit thread from 2023. A Discord server nobody can find. A group chat where someone maybe knows someone. For the most committed format in fantasy football, the entry process is completely ad hoc — no central place to post open spots, no way to search open leagues, no system at all. You either get lucky or you don't play.

What's coming

A purpose-built tool where commissioners post open spots and players find them. Searchable, filterable, and not buried on page four of a subreddit. If you have an orphan team or an open expansion spot, you'll be able to list it. If you want in on a dynasty league, you'll be able to find one.
